Anomaly Instance Segmentation Benchmark


Anomaly Instance Segmentation Benchmark is a brand new benchmark, addressing the challenging problem of instance segmentation

  • Combine 2 benchmarks: SegmentMeIfYouCan and Fishyscapes Lost & Found
  • 3 Datasets: RoadAnomaly21, RoadObstacle21 and Fishyscapes Lost & Found
